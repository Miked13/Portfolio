About Me

Mike Diallo comes from Corvalis, Oregon and graduated from Savannah State University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. At Savannah State, Mike attended the International Student's Association as a way to participate in extracurricular activities, where he learned how to organise a parade.

After graduation, Mike did some internship overseas before moving to Atlanta, GA where he is motivated to start a career in the tech industry. Outside of studies or job, he likes to watch netflix movies or tv series, anime and soccer games. From time to time he also plays video games with his friends online.
